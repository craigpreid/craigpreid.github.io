Cloudiness vs. Latitude

This graph clearly shows that temperatures were higher between -40 and 20 degrees north. Because data was collected at the beginning of December, still during fall, the story here is that temperatures in December drop at higher latitudes, because the northern hemisphere is transitioning into winter. The graph also shows higher temperatures in lower altitudes, because the southern hemisphere is transitioning into summer.

This analysis is set to confirm or debunk this old belief as well as to provide facts around what truly happens to the weather as we approach the equator. The winds and oceans distrbute the heat more evenly.
